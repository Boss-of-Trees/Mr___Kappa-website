My Philosophy

I believe every waiter has to be an unique experience for the guests

I always try to put as much humor into the interaction with guests as somehow possible. With this they feel way more pleasant in comparison to if they would have just been treated by a normal waiter. They feel more engaged and are thus forming an emotional connection with the restaurant. This can result in more frequent visits or even an increased willingness to stay longer and spend more. This of course helps the overall economic situation of the restaurant, but also can have positive effects on the guests psyche.
